Delete Account

If you want to delete your MRI Protocol Assist account, please follow the steps below:

Steps to Delete Your Account

  1. Open the MRI Protocol Assist app
  2. Login to your account
  3. Go to Profile / Settings
  4. Select "Delete Account"
  5. Confirm your request

Data Deletion

Once your account is deleted:

  • Your personal data will be permanently removed
  • Your clinical inputs and usage data will be deleted
  • This action cannot be undone

Request via Email

If you are unable to delete your account from the app, you can request deletion by contacting us:

Email: sadwikkumarsadwik@gmail.com

Processing Time

Account deletion requests will be processed within 3–5 working days.
